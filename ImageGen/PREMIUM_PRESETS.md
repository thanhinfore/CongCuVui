## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Premium Presets

### 1. Letter Spacing is Critical
- **Negative spacing (-2.5 to -0.5):** Modern, tight, impactful
- **Zero spacing (0):** Neutral, balanced
- **Positive spacing (0.5 to 4):** Luxury, elegant, spacious

### 2. Line Height Matters
- **0.9-1.05:** Ultra tight, maximum impact (headlines only)
- **1.1-1.3:** Modern, clean (most content)
- **1.4-1.6:** Elegant, readable (luxury, editorial)

### 3. Font Weight Strategy
- **300-400:** Sophistication, elegance, luxury
- **500-600:** Professional, corporate
- **700-800:** Modern, bold, confident
- **900:** Maximum impact, athletic, powerful

### 4. Alignment Philosophy
- **Left:** Dynamic, modern, reading-friendly
- **Center:** Classic, balanced, formal
- **Right:** Rarely used, special cases

### 5. Position Strategy
- **Top:** Aggressive, attention-grabbing
- **Middle:** Hero style, maximum focus
- **Bottom:** Standard, safe, versatile
